## description of the game
Introduction
SkyClash: Arena of Legends is a fast-paced 2D platform fighting game inspired by competitive arena brawlers such as Brawlhalla. The game places players in floating arenas high above the clouds, where victory is achieved not by draining health bars, but by launching opponents off the stage using precise attacks, combos, and knockback mechanics.
Each match is an intense test of reflexes, timing, and positioning. Players must master movement, aerial combat, and weapon usage while avoiding being knocked out of the arena. As damage increases, characters become more vulnerable to knockback, making every hit increasingly dangerous. The goal is simple yet challenging: outplay your opponents and remain the last fighter standing.

Objectives of the Game
The main objective of SkyClash: Arena of Legends is to defeat opponents by knocking them out of the arena using a combination of attacks, movement skills, and environmental awareness. Instead of traditional health depletion, players accumulate damage percentages that increase the knockback force they receive.
Players are encouraged to develop skillful play through accurate attacks, well-timed dodges, and strategic use of weapons and abilities. Winning requires not only aggression, but also smart positioning and defensive awareness to avoid being launched off the stage.

Gameplay Mechanics
The core gameplay revolves around platform-based combat and knockback physics. Players can move freely across platforms, jump, dodge, and attack in multiple directions. Combat includes light attacks, heavy attacks, aerial strikes, and weapon-based abilities.
As players take damage, their knockback resistance decreases, making them easier to launch off the arena. Falling beyond the stage boundaries results in a knockout, reducing the player’s remaining lives. Matches continue until only one player remains.
Different arenas feature floating platforms and varying layouts, forcing players to adapt their strategies and movement styles during combat.

Game Story
In a mythical sky realm known as the Aether Heights, warriors from different lands are summoned to compete in the legendary SkyClash Tournament. These fighters, known as Skybound Champions, battle to prove their strength, agility, and combat mastery.
The floating arenas are ancient battlegrounds suspended by powerful runes. Falling from these platforms means being cast back into the clouds below. Only the most skilled warrior can survive the chaos and earn the title of Arena Legend.
